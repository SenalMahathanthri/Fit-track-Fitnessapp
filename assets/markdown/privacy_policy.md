# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 5, 2025**

Thank you for using our app! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 1. What Information Do We Collect?

- **Personal Information:** Name, email address (if you provide it via forms).
- **Usage Data:** Device type, app usage statistics, and crash reports.
- **Sensitive Information:** We do **not** collect sensitive personal information.

## 2. How Do We Use Your Information?

- To provide and improve app functionality.
- To communicate with you (if you contact us).
- For analytics and troubleshooting.

## 3. How Do We Store and Protect Your Information?

- Data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel.
- We use encryption and regular security reviews to protect your data.

## 4. Do We Share Your Information?

- We do **not** sell or rent your personal information.
- Data may be shared with service providers (such as analytics or crash reporting tools) only as necessary.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

- You can request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us at [support@yourapp.com](mailto:support@yourapp.com).
- You can opt out of analytics by adjusting your device settings.

## 6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating this page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at [support@yourapp.com](mailto:support@yourapp.com).
